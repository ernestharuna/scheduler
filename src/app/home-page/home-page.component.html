About us

Association Of Badminton Clubs In Nigeria - (ABCIN)

ABCIN is a badminton association which comprises of various badminton clubs, which provide exceptional training schedules, matches, tournaments for out players in eeryclub under an organised environment, we play badminton not just as a game.

This usually doesn't attract a special fee. We offer this in a single package you pay for.

RULE:
1) The players must stand with feet firm on the floor until serve is delivered.
2) When serve is delivered the head of the shuttle must be below the server's waist T the point of contact.
3) The serve must not be delivered until receiver is ready (A receiver is considered ready when attempting to return the shot).
4) A serve must fall into the appropriate service court to be taken as a legal serve.
5) A fake or a balk move from a player is considered an illegal.
6) A serve must fall into the right service court if it falls out of bounds or a short service line it is considered a service fault.
